F a c i l i t i e s  &  S e r v i c e s

Facilities 
Infinity swimming pool (approx 10.5 x 4.5 meters, 1.6 mt deep), outdoor shower/changing area, outdoor grill, 24/7 DSL Internet access, fax, phone, intercom system to communicate between floors and with the pool area, piped in stereo system, TV (in upstairs living room), dishwasher in the kitchen upstairs, and a utility room with washer & dryer and sink. We provide high thread count linen for the bedrooms, soft towels, soap, shampoo and toothpaste for the bathrooms, towels for the beach and the pool, kitchen detergent. All you need to bring is your toothbrush. We also have our own electronic guidebook with suggested beaches, restaurants and shops in the area.  

Services 
English speaking manager to meet you and on call if needed. Weekly cleaning (daily if requested and at an additional charge) and weekly change of linen. Upon request and additional cost, we can arrange for parking, special cooking class or classes conducted by a Neapolitan cook and expert teacher of the local cuisine, or we can have her prepare home-style meals in our kitchen. If desired, we can also have fresh croissants and local pastry such as sfogliatelle delivered daily.
